DYBALA LAUNCHES NEW OFFICIAL
WEBSITE

Paulo Dybala has launched his new official website, paulodybala21.com,
as well as a new logo, the #dybalamask.
Taking on connotations of a warrior’s helmet, La Joya has already adopted
the #dybalamask for several goal celebrations this year.

The website is available in three languages: English, Spanish and Italian,
but in the coming months it will also be translated to accommodate
Dybala’s growing fan base in China and southeast Asia.
It’s no coincidence that the principal colours used are the black and white
of Juventus and the the light blue of his native Argentina.
So head now to paulodybala21.com. La Joya is waiting for you!
